<p><p>The Fabric Relaxing Machine market is categorized into two primary types: Forward Rotation and Forward and Reverse Rotation. Forward Rotation machines primarily facilitate the unrolling and relaxing of fabrics in a single direction, ensuring efficiency in processing. In contrast, Forward and Reverse Rotation machines provide enhanced versatility by allowing fabric movement in both directions, accommodating various fabric types and improving relaxation quality. This flexibility caters to diverse industry needs, promoting better fabric handling and reducing wrinkles during preparation for subsequent processing stages.</p></p>
