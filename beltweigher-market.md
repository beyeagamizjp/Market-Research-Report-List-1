<p><ul><li>Single-idler Beltweigher</li><li>Multi-idler Beltweigher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Beltweigher market is categorized into two main types: Single-idler and Multi-idler beltweighers. Single-idler beltweighers consist of one measuring idler, ideal for applications requiring basic weight measurement with minimal space. They are cost-effective and simple to install. In contrast, Multi-idler beltweighers use multiple idlers for enhanced accuracy and reliability, making them suitable for high-capacity and complex processes. This type allows for better load distribution and can accommodate varying material flows, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
