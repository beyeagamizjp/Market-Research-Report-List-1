<p><p>The emboli removal catheter market is categorized mainly into single lumen and double lumen catheters. Single lumen catheters feature a single channel for both aspiration and retrieval of embolic materials, providing simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, double lumen catheters have separate channels for aspiration and infusion, allowing for more versatile functionality during procedures. Both types are critical in minimally invasive treatments for conditions such as stroke, ensuring effective removal of obstructions while minimizing vascular damage.</p></p>
