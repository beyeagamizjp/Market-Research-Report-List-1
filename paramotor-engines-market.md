<p><p>The paramotor engines market is primarily divided into two categories: 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. 2-stroke engines are lighter, simpler, and often favored for their high power-to-weight ratio, making them suitable for recreational pilots. They typically produce more noise and emissions. In contrast, 4-stroke engines are heavier but offer greater fuel efficiency, lower emissions, and quieter operation, appealing to those seeking longer flights and smoother performance. Both types cater to different preferences and flying styles within the paramotoring community.</p></p>
