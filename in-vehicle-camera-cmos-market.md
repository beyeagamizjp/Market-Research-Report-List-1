<p><ul><li>Parallel Type</li><li>Serial Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-vehicle camera CMOS market is primarily divided into two types: parallel and serial. Parallel type CMOS cameras process multiple bits of data simultaneously, allowing for high-speed image capture and reduced latency, making them ideal for real-time applications like advanced driver-assistance systems. In contrast, serial type CMOS cameras transmit data sequentially, which simplifies wiring and reduces design complexity, but may have slower data transfer rates. Both types cater to different automotive needs, balancing performance and integration ease.</p></p>
