<p><p>Cloud-based Content Management Services (CMS) are categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Private Cloud**: This model offers exclusive access to resources for a single organization, providing enhanced security and control over sensitive data and compliance.</p><p>2. **Public Cloud**: In this setup, services are delivered over the internet to multiple users, offering scalability and cost-efficiency but with shared infrastructure.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Cloud**: This combines both private and public clouds, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of both environments, optimizing flexibility and resource allocation.</p></p>
