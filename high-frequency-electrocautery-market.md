<p><strong>The High Frequency Electrocautery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-frequency electrocautery market comprises two primary types: monopolar and bipolar circuits. Monopolar circuits utilize a single electrode to deliver current through the patient, allowing for cutting and coagulation over larger areas, commonly used in surgeries. In contrast, bipolar circuits involve two electrodes, allowing current to pass between them, providing precise tissue control with minimal thermal spread, ideal for delicate procedures. Both types enhance surgical efficiency and patient safety, catering to varied medical requirements in hospitals and clinics.</p></p>
