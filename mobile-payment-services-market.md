<p><p>Mobile payment services can be categorized into two main market types: remote and proximity. Remote payment refers to transactions conducted over the internet, allowing users to make purchases from anywhere using their mobile devices without physical interaction. This method is ideal for online shopping and service subscriptions. Conversely, proximity payment involves transactions made in person, typically through near-field communication (NFC) technologies, enabling customers to pay by tapping their devices at point-of-sale terminals, enhancing convenience for in-store purchases.</p></p>
