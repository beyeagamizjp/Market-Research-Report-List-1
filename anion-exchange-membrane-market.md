<p><p>The Anion Exchange Membrane market is categorized into two main types: Strong Base and Weak Base anion exchange membranes. Strong Base membranes, typically made from quaternary ammonium groups, deliver high selectivity and conductivity for efficient ion transport in various applications, including water treatment and fuel cells. Weak Base membranes, on the other hand, contain functional groups that can only partially ionize, resulting in lower conductivity but enhanced selectivity in specific separation processes. Both types cater to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
