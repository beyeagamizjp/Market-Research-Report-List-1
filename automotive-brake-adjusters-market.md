<p><p>The automotive brake adjusters market comprises two main types: manual brake adjusters and automatic brake adjusters. Manual brake adjusters require physical intervention to maintain proper brake shoe clearance, allowing for precise adjustments based on specific driving conditions. In contrast, automatic brake adjusters automatically adjust the brake shoes as they wear down, ensuring consistent performance without the need for regular maintenance. These systems enhance safety and efficiency, catering to various vehicle types and consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
