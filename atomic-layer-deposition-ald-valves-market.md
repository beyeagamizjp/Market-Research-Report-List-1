<p><p>The Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) valves market comprises two primary types: pneumatic actuated and electric actuated valves. Pneumatic actuated valves utilize compressed air to open and close, offering fast response times and reliable operation, ideal for high-throughput applications. On the other hand, electric actuated valves rely on electrical signals for actuation, providing precise control and ease of integration with automated systems. Both types are essential for ensuring accurate gas flow and maintaining system integrity in ALD processes.</p></p>
