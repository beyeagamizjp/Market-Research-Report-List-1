<p><p>The Civil Combustible Gas Alarm market comprises two primary types: fixed and portable combustible gas alarms. Fixed combustible gas alarms are installed permanently in buildings, designed to continuously monitor for gas leaks and provide real-time alerts. They are often integrated into building safety systems. In contrast, portable combustible gas alarms are handheld devices that can be used in various locations, making them ideal for on-site inspections or emergency response. Both types ensure safety by detecting potentially hazardous gas levels.</p></p>
