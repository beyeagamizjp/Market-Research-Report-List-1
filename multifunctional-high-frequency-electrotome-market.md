<p><strong>The Multifunctional High Frequency Electrotome Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multifunctional High Frequency Electrotome market encompasses two primary circuit types: monopolar and bipolar. The monopolar circuit utilizes a single electrode for both cutting and coagulation, requiring a grounding pad for effective energy delivery, making it suitable for larger tissue areas. In contrast, the bipolar circuit employs two electrodes to deliver radiofrequency energy directly to the tissue, allowing for precise cutting and coagulation with minimal thermal spread. Each type serves different surgical needs, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
