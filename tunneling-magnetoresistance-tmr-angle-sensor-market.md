<p><p>The Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) angle sensor market is categorized into two primary types: digital output and analog output. Digital output TMR sensors provide discrete signals, typically in binary form, indicating the angle of magnetic fields with high precision and noise immunity, making them suitable for digital systems. In contrast, analog output TMR sensors deliver continuous signals proportional to the angle measurement, enabling smoother readings and fine resolution in applications where gradual changes need to be monitored.</p></p>
