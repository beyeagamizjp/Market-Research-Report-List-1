<p><p>The satellite-based narrow-band communication market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Receiver** - Devices that decode signals transmitted from satellites for various applications.</p><p>2. **Transmitter/Transponder** - Equipment that sends signals to satellites, crucial for data transmission.</p><p>3. **Transceiver** - Integrated units combining both transmitting and receiving functions for efficient communication.</p><p>4. **Antenna** - Essential components that facilitate signal reception and transmission between the satellite and ground systems.</p><p>5. **Others** - This category includes ancillary equipment like signal processors and multiplexers that support narrow-band communication systems.</p></p>
