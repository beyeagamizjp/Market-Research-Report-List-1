<p><p>Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) are semiconductor lasers that emit light from their edges, prevalent in telecommunications and data communications. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Distributed Feedback Laser (DFB)** - offers single-mode operation with high wavelength stability.</p><p>2. **Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser (DBR)** - features a wavelength-selective reflector, crucial for precise applications.</p><p>3. **Fabry-Perot Laser** - provides multi-mode output, commonly used in lower-cost applications.</p><p>4. **Broad Area Laser Diodes** - deliver high output power over large areas, suitable for industrial and medical uses.</p></p>
