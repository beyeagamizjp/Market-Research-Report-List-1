<p><p>The liquid mold silicone rubber market is divided into two primary types: one-component and two-component silicone rubber. One-component silicone rubber is ready to use and cures when exposed to moisture in the air, making it convenient for smaller applications and easy handling. In contrast, two-component silicone rubber requires mixing a base component with a curing agent before use. This type typically offers enhanced performance and durability, making it suitable for more demanding applications, such as industrial mold-making and intricate designs.</p></p>
