<p><p>The tattoo removal laser equipment market consists of three main types: gas, liquid, and solid laser equipment. Gas laser equipment, such as argon or carbon dioxide lasers, utilizes gas mediums to emit energy, offering precise removal through thermal effects. Liquid laser equipment employs dye-based technology that generates laser light, focusing on various ink colors for effective removal. Solid laser equipment, often using solid-state crystals like neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet (Nd:YAG), provides high-intensity beams suitable for diverse tattoo ink types and skin tones.</p></p>
