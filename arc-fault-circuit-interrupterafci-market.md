<p><p>Arc Fault Circuit Interrupters (AFCIs) are designed to detect dangerous electrical arcs that could lead to fires. The market comprises various types: Branch/Feeder AFCIs protect entire circuits from arcs, while Combination AFCIs (CAFCI) offer dual protection by sensing both series and parallel arcs. Outlet AFCIs are installed at outlets for localized protection. Other types may include portable AFCIs or devices integrated into smart home systems. Together, these products enhance electrical safety by minimizing fire risks associated with faulty wiring.</p></p>
