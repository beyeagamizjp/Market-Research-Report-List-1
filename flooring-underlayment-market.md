<p><p>The flooring underlayment market comprises various types that enhance flooring performance and comfort. Cement Backer Units (CBU) provide moisture resistance for tile installations, while polyethylene offers a cost-effective moisture barrier. Rubber underlayment is known for its sound absorption properties, making it ideal for noise reduction in multi-story buildings. Cork is a sustainable option, providing thermal insulation and cushioning. Plywood serves as a sturdy, flat base, improving stability for various flooring types. Each type serves specific needs in flooring applications.</p></p>
