<p><p>The household massage chair market can be categorized into two main types: rail massage chairs and trackless massage chairs. Rail massage chairs utilize a fixed rail system that guides the massage mechanism along a specific path, providing targeted relief in predefined areas. Trackless massage chairs, on the other hand, do not rely on a fixed track, allowing for a more versatile and customizable massage experience as they can move freely across various body contours. Both types cater to different user preferences and comfort levels.</p></p>
