<p><strong>The Rapid Thermal Annealing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid Thermal Annealing (RTA) equipment is primarily categorized into lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTA utilizes intense infrared lamps for rapid heating, offering uniform temperature distribution and quick processing times, making it ideal for semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, laser-based RTA employs focused laser beams to achieve localized heating, allowing for precise control and tailoring of thermal treatments. This method often results in minimal thermal stress on substrates, making it suitable for advanced materials and delicate structures.</p></p>
