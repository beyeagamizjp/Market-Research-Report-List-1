<p><p>The rubber urinary catheter market comprises two main types: intermittent urinary catheters and indwelling urinary catheters. Intermittent catheters are designed for temporary use, allowing patients to catheterize as needed, which helps reduce the risk of infection and maintain independence. Indwelling catheters, on the other hand, are intended for longer-term use, remaining in place for extended periods to continuously drain urine. Both types play crucial roles in managing urinary retention and other medical conditions, catering to varying patient needs.</p></p>
