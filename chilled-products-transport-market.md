<p><strong>The Chilled Products Transport Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Compression Systems</li><li>Cryogenic Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chilled products transport market encompasses technologies used to maintain low temperatures during the transportation of perishable goods. Vapor compression systems utilize refrigerants, compressing them to create a cooling effect, commonly found in refrigerated trucks and containers. Cryogenic systems, on the other hand, employ liquefied gases like nitrogen or argon to achieve significantly lower temperatures, ideal for transporting sensitive materials such as pharmaceuticals or certain foods. Both systems ensure product integrity and safety throughout the transport process.</p></p>
