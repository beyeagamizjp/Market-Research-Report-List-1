<p><p>Surface computing encompasses various market types based on interaction dimensions. Two-dimensional markets focus on flat surfaces, like tablets and touchscreens, enabling users to engage with content through gestures and multi-touch capabilities. In contrast, three-dimensional markets extend interaction into spatial environments, utilizing technologies like holograms and spatial computing. Here, users can manipulate digital objects within a 3D space, enhancing immersion and facilitating more intuitive interactions. Both types aim to create seamless user experiences by blending digital and physical realms.</p></p>
