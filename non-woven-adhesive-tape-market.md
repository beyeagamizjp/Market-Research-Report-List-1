<p><p>The non-woven adhesive tape market comprises three main types: acrylic, rubber, and silicone. Acrylic tapes are known for their strong bonding and weather resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Rubber tapes offer excellent adhesive properties and are often used for packaging and general bonding. Silicone tapes provide high-temperature resistance and are used in specialized applications like electrical insulation. Each type serves distinct needs across industries, ensuring versatility in usage for various bonding and sealing requirements.</p></p>
