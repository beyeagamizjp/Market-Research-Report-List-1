<p><p>The capacitance meter market is primarily categorized into non-bridge and bridge meters. Non-bridge meters are simpler devices designed for direct capacitance measurements, often used for general purpose applications in electronic testing. In contrast, bridge meters utilize a more complex design that allows for highly accurate capacitance measurements by balancing an electrical bridge circuit. This makes bridge meters ideal for precision tasks in laboratory settings and high-end applications, offering enhanced performance and versatility compared to non-bridge models.</p></p>
