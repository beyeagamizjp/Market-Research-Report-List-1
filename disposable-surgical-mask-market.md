<p><ul><li>Three Layers Non-woven Mask</li><li>Four Layers Non-woven Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable surgical mask market primarily features two types: the three-layer non-woven mask and the four-layer non-woven mask. The three-layer mask typically consists of an outer hydrophobic layer, a middle melt-blown filter layer, and an inner moisture-absorbing layer, offering basic protection against droplets and pathogens. The four-layer mask adds an extra filtration layer, enhancing its protective capabilities for healthcare settings. Both types are designed for single use, ensuring hygiene and minimizing cross-contamination in various environments.</p></p>
