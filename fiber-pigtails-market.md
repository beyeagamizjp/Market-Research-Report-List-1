<p><p>The Fiber Pigtails market is segmented into single fiber pigtails and multi-fiber pigtails. Single fiber pigtails consist of a single optical fiber that connects one device or component to a network, commonly used for simple, point-to-point connections. Multi-fiber pigtails, on the other hand, contain multiple fibers housed within a single cable, enabling higher bandwidth and efficient data transmission over longer distances. This variety caters to diverse applications in telecommunications, data centers, and industrial networking environments.</p></p>
