<p><strong>The Online Ammonia Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric</li><li>Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE)</li><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online ammonia analyzers market comprises three primary types: colorimetric, ion-selective electrodes (ISE), and ultraviolet (UV) absorbance. Colorimetric analyzers utilize chemical reactions to produce a color change, indicating ammonia concentration. Ion-selective electrodes detect ammonia ions directly through selective measurement, offering high specificity. UV absorbance analyzers measure the absorbance of light at specific wavelengths, correlating it to ammonia levels. Each type serves distinct applications in environmental monitoring, wastewater treatment, and industrial processes, emphasizing accuracy and real-time data for effective decision-making.</p></p>
