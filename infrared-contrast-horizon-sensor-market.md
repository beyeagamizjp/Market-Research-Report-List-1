<p><p>The Infrared Contrast Horizon Sensor market is segmented into two primary types: Active and Passive Infrared Contrast Horizon Sensors. Active sensors emit infrared radiation and measure the reflected signals to determine the horizon, providing enhanced accuracy in various conditions. On the other hand, Passive sensors rely on detecting natural infrared radiation emitted from objects and surfaces in the environment, making them ideal for energy-efficient applications. Both types are crucial in aerospace, robotics, and autonomous vehicles for navigation and obstacle detection.</p></p>
