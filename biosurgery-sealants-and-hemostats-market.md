<p><p>The biosurgery sealants and hemostats market comprises three main types: hemostats, internal tissue sealants, and adhesion barriers. Hemostats are used to control bleeding during surgical procedures, promoting rapid clot formation. Internal tissue sealants serve to close internal wounds or surgical incisions, enhancing tissue integration and healing. Adhesion barriers are designed to prevent the formation of adhesions, which can occur post-surgery and complicate recovery. Together, these products improve surgical outcomes and patient safety by enhancing tissue management.</p></p>
